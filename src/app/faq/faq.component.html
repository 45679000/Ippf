Frequently Asked Questions

You will be able to access and view the dataset without logging in, but when you decide to download a dataset, you will need to to the Data Sharing Portal.

For the user registration, you will need to register and create a password-protected account.

The datasets included are:

  • Client Exit Interviews
  • Service Statistics
  • Impact Data

The service statistics consists of data from various public and private health facilities which is compiled monthly by participating countries and aggregated at a central level for reporting purposes.
Impact data is calculated quarterly (and annually) at the central level from the service statistics and Impact 2.5.

Client Exit Interview data is collected annually in each country in a sample of public and private facilities that report into the service statistics data. Specific variables are then combined for program reporting and adaptive learning.

Once you have registered to the WISH Data Sharing portal, you will have your own account, which can be accessed by logging in.

The datasets can be downloaded from their page.
Please include the following information in your request:
  • Name
  • Email
  • Organisation
  • Country
  • Purpose of data use

Users can browse through the data catalogue, search for the data using the filters available on the portal, browse through organisations, and find the datasets they are interested in.

Requests to access datasets will be approved within 48hours. Once your request has been approved, you will receive an email with instructions on how the data can be accessed/downloaded.

The time allotted for the use of the data is 6 months. If you want to use the same data after the six months, you will need to submit a new request.

Researchers, learning institutions, and organisations may register on the WISH data sharing portal to access and obtain data. Some datasets have restriction on access due to data confidentiality.

You can use the search filter to get an overview of datasets that are open and restricted.

Users can leave comments and reviews on the feedback section located at the footer menu of the Data Portal, or use the contacts found at the ‘Contact us’ section still in the footer menu.
