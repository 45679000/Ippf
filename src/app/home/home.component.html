IPPF DATA Sharing Portal

Key Data from the WISH2ACTION Programme

The WISH Data and Resource Portal provides data resources for Sexual and Reproductive Health (SRH) to researchers, policy makers, program planners and institutions.

This portal provides users with data from the WISH2ACTION programme in 11 countries: Burundi, Chad, DRC, Ethiopia, Malawi, Mauritania, Somalia, Sudan, South Sudan, Uganda, and Tanzania. The portal enables users to access published country data sets of the key SRH indicators. Users are encouraged to explore the data and develop customized tables, charts, graphs, and maps based on their objectives.
